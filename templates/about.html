Our Technology

CineMatch uses state-of-the-art content-based filtering techniques powered by machine learning. Our recommendation engine analyzes movie attributes including genres, cast, directors, plot descriptions, and more to calculate similarity scores and suggest movies you'll love. With a database of over 3,000 movies and growing, we're constantly improving our algorithms to serve you better.
